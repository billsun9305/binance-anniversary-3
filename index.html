“Off the Charts!” Virtual Conference

On July 14, 2020, join Binance as we kick off our third anniversary with one of the biggest blockchain events of the year.
Get the latest news and updates on all things blockchain and crypto, and take an exclusive look at what’s coming next at our blockbuster 10-hour live event. With multi-regional programming, this unique event brings together 80+ influential speakers, including leading blockchain and crypto innovators, business and technology leaders, influential academics, and key policymakers.
Join our can’t-miss event with powerful talks, breakthrough panels, opportunities to win prizes, and much, much more.

Binance Awards 2020

Join Binance as we celebrate the standout innovators and businesses
that have made sizable contributions, both to our community and to our blockchain ecosystem.
Winners will be announced during our live event, and results will be published on our blog afterward.

Category Awards

Binance Influencer Awards 2020 (Global & Regional)

This is an award that is determined by our community. We appreciate all influencers who contribute to evangelizing, educating, and elevating crypto and blockchain to the public!

Nomination

Forms will be shared with our communities globally and regionally to collect nominations.

Selection criteria

The top 5 nominations will undergo a second round of community votes to determine the influencer of the year.

Binance’s Angels of the Year 2020 (Global & China)

Our Angel volunteers are the pillars of our community! We salute to all our Angel’s contributions to our community that has been the drives of our growth.

Nomination

All existing Binance official angels with more than 6 months are automatically nominated

Selection criteria

Angel peer vote, Binance team vote, and Angel manager vote will contribute ⅓ weight each to determine the winners for the five Angel specialties.

Best Binance Chain Wallet 2020 (Global)

This is an award that is 100% determined by Binance Chain users’ daily usage. Wallets are the portal to help users interact with Binance Chain. We appreciate all 30 wallets who contribute to the user experience of Binance Chain!

Nomination

All types of wallets which support Binance Chain mainnet or testnet, excluding Web wallet(binance.org)

Selection criteria

Popularity, Technicality, Originality, and the potential positive impact for the Binance Chain ecosystem. The selection is witnessed by validators.

Binance’s Launchpad Project of the Year 2020 (Global)

This is an award for the Launchpad project that has yielded a high return for investors and contributed significantly to the Binance Ecosystem.

Nomination

All Launchpad projects between July 2019 to July 2020, excluding Binance portfolio projects, qualify for nomination.

Selection criteria

Market ROI performance and level of integration with the Binance ecosystem (eg. supporting and involvement with BNB, BUSD, Binance Chain, staking/savings, listings on Binance affiliate exchanges, and joint marketing campaigns with Binance) will determine the winner.

Binance’s Philanthropist of the Year 2020 (Global)

This is an award to highlight the significant impact made by philanthropists in the blockchain community, who have partnered with Binance Charity to advance blockchain-powered philanthropy.

Nomination

The Binance Charity team will nominate the top 5 donors who have contributed the most to our charitable projects since 2018.

Selection criteria

The winner will be selected based on community vote by Binance Charity Twitter followers. Voting criteria include the total value of donations made through Binance Charity, project engagement frequency, and impact in global sustainable development.
